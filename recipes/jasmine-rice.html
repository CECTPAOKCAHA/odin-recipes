Instant Pot Jasmine Rice

Description:

Basic jasmine rice recipe.

Ingredients:

For 2 servings

  • Jasmine rice - 1 cup;
  • Water - 1 cup.

Steps:

  1. Put all the ingredients into the Instant Pot and cook on medium pressure for 3 mins.
  2. Allow 10 min of Natural Release.
