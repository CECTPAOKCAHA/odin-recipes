Banana Pancakes

Ingredients:

Banana - 2 each;
Plant milk or water - 1 cup;
Baking powder - 3 tsp;
Flax seed, ground - 2 tbsp;
Buckwheat flour - 1.5 cups;
Maple syrup - 2 tbsp;

Directions:

Mix all the ingredients and fry on dry pan on heat setting '4', 4 mins covered, then flip and fry another 4 mins uncovered.
