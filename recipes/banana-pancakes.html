Banana Pancakes

Description:

You probably won't impress anyone with the taste of these, but if your focus is functional, healthy and sustainable nutrition, it's a really nice way to make pancakes

Ingredients:

  • Banana - 2 each;
  • Plant milk or water - 1 cup;
  • Baking powder - 3 tsp;
  • Flax seed, ground - 2 tbsp;
  • Buckwheat flour - 1.5 cups;
  • Maple syrup - 2 tbsp;

Steps:

  1. Puree the bananas with a fork or with a blender.
  2. Mix all wet ingredients.
  3. Add the ground flax seet to the wet mix and let sit for 5 mins.
  4. Mix all dry ingredients.
  5. Mix wet and dry mixes until just incorporated, do not overmix.
  6. Fry the pancakes on dry pan on heat setting '4', for 4 mins covered.
  7. Flip and fry another 4 mins uncovered.
