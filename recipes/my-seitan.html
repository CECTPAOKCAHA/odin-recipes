My Seitan (More than 70 g of protein per serving).

Description:

Seitan is a high-protein food that absorbs the flavour of its sauce and therefore very versatile.

Ingredients:

For 4 servings

  • Vital Gluten - 270 g
  • Pea or hemp isolated protein powder - 120 g
  • Soy sauce - 4 tbsp
  • Liquid smoke - 1 tsp
  • Nutmeg ground - 1 tsp
  • Allspice ground - tsp
  • Water - 1.5 cups (might need to adjust depending on the humidity)

Steps:

  1. Mix all the ingredients to yield a rubbery dough ball that doesn't stick to your fingers.
  2. Use Instant Pot steamer, steam for 20 minunes on medium pressure setting.
  3. Cut into stripes and further stir fry or barbeque, or use in stews. You can also steam it on the stove using a traditional steamer, but you will need to cut the dough ball into smaller pieces and steam for 40 mins.
