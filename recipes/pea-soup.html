Instant Pot Pea Soup

Description:

Super-simple and hearty, this is our go-to soup when we don't know what to cook.

Ingredients:

For 2 servings

  • Dried peas - 1 cup;
  • Brown rice - 1/4 cup;
  • Water - 7 cups;
  • Onion soup from concentrate - 1 package OR Veggie bouillion cube - 1 cube;
  • Crushed chilli pepper - 1 tsp;
  • Dried estragon - 2 tsp.

Steps:

  1. Put all the ingredients into the Instant Pot and cook on medium pressure for 20 mins.
  2. Allow for pressure valve to release itself (about 20 mins).
