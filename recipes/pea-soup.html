Instant Pot Pea Soup

Ingredients:

Dried peas - 1 cup;
Brown rice - 1/4 cup;
Water - 7 cups;
Onion soup from concentrate - 1 package OR Veggie bouillion cube - 1 cube;
Crushed chilli pepper - 1 tsp;
Dried estragon - 2 tsp.

Directions:

Put all the ingredients into the Instant Pot and cook on medium pressure for 20 mins. Allow for pressure valve to release itself (about 20 mins).
